Some Things About Us

It's All About the Kids

Healthy Meals


Guarding Your Angels offers meals that are catered in. All meals meet the CACFP child meal pattern guidelines.


Diversity


We are a bilingual center that teaches in English and Spanish. We incorporate the spanish language through music, books, numbers, and art.

Mindful Child Care


Children love to explore the world around them, and we want to provide them a peaceful and creative journey of life. We provide our children with different mindful activities throughout the day like: yoga, breathing exercises, and awareness practices. Our children will learn to be in touch with their internal and external environments to cultivate social and emotional understanding.



ClassDojo Tracking


At Guarding Your Angels we incorporate an app that allows us to keep track of your children's daily activities and communication with teacher and administration. This app is very useful for you to feel like your involved in your childs day to day.



High Quality Air Filtration System


Having a quality air filtration will reduce the spread of germs.

COVID-19 Procedures


We follow the CDC guidelines. We disinfect all common spaces daily and toys.
